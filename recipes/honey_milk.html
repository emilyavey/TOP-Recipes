honey milk

This is a very quick and soothing drink you can make at home! The sweet, rich taste of honey combined with the warm, gentle milk creates a humble drink that anyone can find comfort in. Bonus points: you can use any milk alternative and it's just as tasty without the stomach discomfort!

ingredients

Note: The recipe requires very little and emphasizes the tastes of the honey and milk, so it's advised to use yummy versions of both core ingredients. If you've been hoarding some delicious honey, now's the time to use it!

  • Milk
  • Honey

instructions

  1. You can heat up the milk via stovetop until just before boiling (you don't want to burn the milk!) or by putting a cup (any general cup, not the measurement) and heating it up in the microwave. I tend to leave my cup in the microwave for about 1 minute and 30 seconds.
  2. Once the milk is a little too hot to drink, stir in your desired amount of honey. When the milk is hot enough, the honey will have an easier time dissolving. Keep adding honey until you like the taste of the drink.

that's it, enjoy your fresh drink :)
