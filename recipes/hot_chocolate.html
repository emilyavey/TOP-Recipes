How to Make Hot Chocolate

This is how to make a rich and simple hot chocolate! A nice and simple drink to have on any cold day.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 to 2 tbsp Sugar
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. In a small saucepan over medium heat, whisk together the cocoa powder, milk, sugar, and salt.
  2. Add in the vanilla after everything is incorporated and serve.

That's it! I hope you enjoy your fresh hot chocolate :)
